Our History

Aini Borcelle aims to deliver the most delectable desserts the culinary world has to offer, which is why we ensure that each and every order that leaves our kitchen, is guaranteed to both visually entice and excite any flavour palate.

The name, Aini Borcelle, was derived from the desire to take modern day desserts and put them on a pedestal of their own, hence, the non-stop pursuit for new methods and combinations of ingredients to create sweets that would catapult taste buds to new heights.
